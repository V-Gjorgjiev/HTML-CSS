FAQ Section

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by calling us, emailing us, visiting our store, or scheduling online through our website

What are the different types of glasses I can purchase?
We offer a wide range of options, including glasses, contact lenses, sunglasses and designer eyewear frames.

What is the best way to choose the right pair of glasses for me?
Our opticians are here to help! Visit our store to try on frames, discuss your needs and get personalized recommendations for both style and functionality.

Do I need to have a prescription for my glasses?
While some glasses, like non-perscription sunglasses, don't require a prescription, we recommend having a professional eye exam to ensure the right lenses for your vision needs.

How often should I perform an eye exam?
We recommend having an eye exam every 1-2 years, or sooner if you experiance any changes in your vision or eye health.

What is the cost of my eye exam?
Eye exam costs vary depanding on the type of exam. Contact us for the pricing details or visit Book an Appointment

Can I cancel my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel your appointment. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule or cancel.

How long does it take to receive my glasses?
Glasses typically take 5-10 business days to be ready, depending on the complexity of your prescription and the type of lenses or frames selected.
